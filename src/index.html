Tip Calc+

tip calculator app

A very simple PWA Tip Calculator based on HTML, CSS and pure JavaScript. This app will help you management your bills and tips when you will spend free time in the restaurants with friends or single for example. It doesn’t matter what type of currency you want to pay because this app uses API and counts from many popular currencies to PL złoty dynamicly.

Key benefits of tip calculator

  • Less problems

    Save time, avoiding mistakes during calculation your bill and tip

  • Handling multi currencies

    Connect with API and count your bills and tips using different kind of currencies to Polish złoty.

  • History of paying your app

    The history of paying is stored in memory of browser you use, so if you refresh browser the data of history doesn’t appear unless you clear your browser memory.

  • Off-line working

    If you won’t have an internet connection the app will be work correctly all time.
